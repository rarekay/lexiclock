Welcome to LexiClock

The Scrabble timer iPhone players have always needed. Free, offline, and built for the game.

Tap to pass the clock

When your turn is done, tap your tile. The clock switches to your opponent instantly. Choose per-turn or total time mode.

Challenge any word

Hit Challenge to pause the clock and check any word — or multiple words at once — against Collins or NWL2023. One clear verdict, no arguments.
